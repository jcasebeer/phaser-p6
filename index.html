Controls:

SNIPER PHONE

  • High Concept:
    YOU FIND A MYSTERIOUS PHONE TELLING YOU TO ASSASSINATE CERTAIN PEOPLE, DO YOU LISTEN???
  • Player Experience Goals:
    THE PLAYER SHOULD RACK THEIR BRAIN AS THEY TRY TO IDENTIFY THEIR TARGET
  • Genre:
    WHERE'S WALDO? TYPE GAME
  • Game play:
    AIM YOUR GUN WITH THE ARROW KEYS AND SHOOT WITH X
    YOU MUST SHOOT THE TARGET MATCHING THE DESCRIPTION
    GIVEN TO YOU BY THE PHONE, DON'T SCREW UP!
    THIS IS JUST A PROTOTYPE, IN THE ACTUAL GAME
    YOU WILL BE TIMED... YOUR TARGET WILL BE ABLE TO
    "GET AWAY". THIS WOULD ADD SOME GOOD TENSION TO THIS
    OTHERWISE TENSIONLESS GAME.
  • Prototype goal:
    MAKE A GAME WHERE SPRITES CAN BE TINTED DIFFERENTLY IN
    MULTIPLE AREAS
  • Features:
    27 DIFFERENT POSSIBLE TARGET COMBINATIONS
    (3 HAT COLORS * 3 SHIRT COLORS * 3 PANT COLORS)
  • Setting/Premise:
    YOU FIND A PHONE, IT TELLS YOU TO SHOOT SOMEONE...
  • Story: N/A
  • Target Audience:
    THIS GAME IS TARGETED AT PEOPLE WHO LIKE TO SHOOT PEOPLE AT THE REQUEST OF A PHONE.
  • Play time:
    UNTIL YOU SCREW UP!
  • Strategies:
    TAKE YOUR TIME, TRY TO KEEP YOUR FIELD OF VISION WIDE SO THAT
    YOU DON'T MISS ANYTHING!
